# Breathe Easy: Lung Health for People Living with HIV (PLWH)

A comprehensive, evidence-based resource kit to support community outreach programs educating PLWH about increased lung cancer risk and providing actionable guidance on smoking cessation.

## Purpose
This repository contains structured resources for healthcare professionals and community workers to deliver effective lung health education and smoking cessation support tailored to People Living with HIV.

## Target Audience
- Healthcare providers (doctors, nurses, clinicians)
- Community health workers and outreach staff
- People Living with HIV (PLWH) seeking reliable information
- Public health educators and program coordinators

## Project Structure
The resource kit is organized into several core components:
1. **Research Compilation** – evidence-based smoking cessation strategies and open-access research papers
2. **Patient‑Facing Materials** – FAQ guides and discussion checklists
3. **Provider Resources** – curated scientific literature for healthcare professionals
4. **Program Implementation Tools** – templates and guidelines for community outreach

## Getting Started
Browse the repository files and issues to explore available resources. Contributions and feedback are welcome to improve the kit's relevance and impact.
